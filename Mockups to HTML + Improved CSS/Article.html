    • Baguio City

    Baguio City, also known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," is a charming and picturesque destination located in the mountainous region of Benguet. It offers a cool and refreshing climate, beautiful landscapes, and a rich cultural heritage.

    One of the most iconic landmarks in Baguio is The Mansion, the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. Surrounded by lush gardens, it is a must-visit spot for history enthusiasts and those seeking Instagram-worthy photo opportunities.

    Another popular attraction is Burnham Park, a sprawling urban park named after American architect Daniel Burnham. Here, you can enjoy boating in the man-made lake, go biking, have a picnic, or simply take a leisurely stroll amidst the vibrant flowers and towering pine trees.

    Baguio is also famous for its strawberries, and you can experience the joy of picking your own fresh strawberries at the Strawberry Farm in La Trinidad, just a short drive away from the city center. Don't forget to try the delicious strawberry taho and strawberry ice cream, which are local favorites!

    Whether you're an adventure seeker, nature lover, or simply looking for a peaceful getaway, Baguio City has something to offer everyone. Its cool climate, breathtaking scenery, and warm hospitality make it a perfect destination to rejuvenate and create lasting memories.
